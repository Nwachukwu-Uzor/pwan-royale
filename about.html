about us

PWAN Royale Investment and Development Limited is a subsidiary of PWAN Group Limited, based in Lekki, Lagos, Nigeria. The name ‘PWAN’ is an acronym for ‘Property World African Network’ (PWAN). We are a group of reliable real estate and property management companies that provides HOME ownership and INVESTMENT opportunities that cater to the needs of anyone desiring to invest in real estate. We are product specialists that work diligently and tirelessly in representing our client’s best interests. We provide tailor-made marketing services and dynamic opportunities in Real Estate industry. We always believe that there’s nothing more important than owning a home at zero Cost. We have proven real estate experience when it comes to the business of selling or buying land and buildings. PWAN Royale Investment and Development Limited is committed to high standards of excellence and integrity. We bring our expertise to every project handled by our experienced team, providing a thorough, qualitative service in alignment with our clients. No matter your pursuit, we’re here to help you find your perfect property.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Our objectives Is to engage efficiently, responsibly and profitably in Real Estate and other selected businesses We participate in the search for and development of property to meet evolving client’s needs and the country’s growing demand for properties.

Our Vision

To make Home ownership dream a reality and to be a global leading real estate company, exceeding client expectations with a tradition of trust and transparency in discovering affordable and secured lands in fast developing areas and making them accessible to our clients.

Our Mission

Provide with royalty, great property for all in a most cost effective manner with peace of mind.

WHAT CAN WE OFFER YOU?

At present, the company’s portfolio includes the provision and development of affordable residential and commercial estates within the Lagos Island and Lagos Mainland axis, Asaba and Warri in Delta State, Umuahia in Abia State, Ibadan, Owerri, Uyo and Port Harcourt with plans to expand to other parts of the metropolis soon.
